Publications

Comparative Study of MPC and RL Controllers for Quadrupedal Robot Locomotion
IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS), 2023
A comprehensive comparative study analyzing Model Predictive Control (MPC) and Reinforcement Learning (RL) controllers on the Unitree Go1 quadrupedal robot. The research addresses controller selection challenges and contributes insights for quadrupedal locomotion in various applications through trajectory analysis and perturbation experiments.
Predictive Sampling Algorithms for Precise Quadruped Locomotion
IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters, 2023
Implementation and validation of predictive sampling algorithms for Model Predictive Control, focusing on refining actions for precise locomotion in quadrupedal robots. The study demonstrates enhanced performance through standardized task validation and trajectory analysis using Python and MATLAB.
Hardware-in-the-Loop Testing for Industrial Automation Systems
International Conference on Automation Science and Engineering, 2023
A detailed analysis of Hardware-in-the-Loop (HIL) testing methodologies for industrial automation systems, including PLC integration with robotic simulation environments. The paper presents practical applications in assembly line automation and communication protocols.
Model-Based Control Design for Hybrid Electric Vehicle Powertrains
SAE Technical Paper Series, 2022
Development and validation of model-based control strategies for hybrid electric vehicle powertrains using Simulink and Stateflow. The research focuses on blending strategies and HIL testing methodologies for automotive applications with emphasis on efficiency optimization.
