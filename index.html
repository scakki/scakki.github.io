Research Interests

My research focuses on the intersection of legged robotics, reinforcement learning (RL), and control systems. I am particularly interested in developing robust control algorithms that enhance robotic locomotion across diverse terrains. By combining cutting-edge techniques in machine learning and controls, my goal is to push the boundaries of what autonomous robotic systems can achieve, particularly in applications such as space exploration and complex terrestrial tasks.
